Sesame Strawberry Jam Focaccia

Description

A sweet twist on the traditionally savory Italian bread. This recipe will take a couple days to complete.

While the ingredients call for strawberry jam and sesame seeds, you can use a wide variety of jams and herbs for different flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • 208 g bread flour
  • 175 ml water
  • 1/2 tbsp (7 ml) sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp (2g) sugar
  • 1/2 tsp (1.6 g) yeast
  • 5 tbsp strawberry jam, divided
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • sesame seeds, for topping
  • flaky salt, for topping

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: In a medium bowl, add water and yeast. Swirl the bowl before adding the sugar and sesame oil to stir. Add flour and salt until it comes together. The dough should be very wet and shaggy. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Stretch and fold: Wet hands and stretch the dough from the top of the bowl, pulling up and then to the other side of the dough. Repeat all the way around the bowl. Cover and let rest for 20 minutes and repeat stretch and fold. Cover and rest for 20 more minutes. Dallop 2 tablespoons of the strawberry jam over the dough. Fold and stretch. Flip dough, drizzle with 1/2 tablespoon olive oil, cover and refigerate overnight.
  3. Next day: Line and oil a 8.25 x 6.25 inch baking pan with the other 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil. Remove dough from fridgeand flip into the pan. Bring top edge downand bottom edge up, folding it over. Flip so seam side is down.
  4. Proof for 1 - 1 1/2 hours. Time will depend on kitchen temperature.
  5. Pre-heat oven to 425 F.
  6. Drizzle with sesame oil and dimple dough. Add the remaining strawberry jam and push jam into dimples. Top with flaky salt and sesame seeds. Bake for 18-22 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a wire rack and cool for 20 minutes before slicing.
